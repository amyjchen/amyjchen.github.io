S E L F

I regularly review and revise my rules of governance. They are listed here to further keep me accountable.

  • Tell the truth and be honest, even in minor circumstances.
  • View others according to how they act, not their reputation.
  • Always think before speaking.
  • Learn as much as you can about everything that you can.
  • Be confident but humble and empathetic. Avoid arrogance at all costs.
  • Be generous.
  • Be present and pay attention. Everyone and anything is interesting and can be learned from, so long as you give it time.
  • Do not shame and do not give weight to shame.
  • Be direct, be bold.
  • Move slow and fix things.
    • Amy Chen 2021
