E M A I L L I N K E D I N I N S T A G R A M T U M B L R

A R T

Installation and interactive artist. Shown work in Stanford's Undergraduate Juried Art Exhibit, the Cannon Tunnel in Washington D.C., Frost Music Festival, and the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards' National Exhibit at Parsons in New York, among others. National awards in the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards and the Congressional Art Competition. Won grand prize in the 8th annual Lucerne Art of Dairy contest. Current projects: #objectaday (daily photo series on Instagram), sunTen (iOS app), and a hand-sanded Gongfu tea table made from a slab of redwood.

R E S U M E W O R K S A W A C O W

W R I T E

Director of Stanford's Spoken Word Collective, former Editor-in-Chief of Leland Quarterly, and occasional contributor to Stanford Arts Review. National medalist in the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards, most notably for a poem composed of derivatives (yes, from calculus). Published in the Stanford Journal of Asian American Studies (Vol. 7) and The Best Teen Writing of 2012. Former student columnist for the Lake Oswego Review. Involved in literary magazines and organiztions since 2010. Current projects: Homewreck'd (tentative title, novella), poetry (as always), weekly-ish blog (on Tumblr).

Passionate about technologies and ideas that improve quality of life and uphold what matters most. Full-stack Search Experience Intern at eBay (Summer 2017). Experienced in Processing, Facebook's Graph API, and using OpenCV in her own art practice, 3DR drones in human-drone interaction research, and Pebble smartwatches (rip) in a watchface project (VIBES).
